Frequently Asked Questions
How do I add a new task? ▶
Click the "+ Quick Add Task" button on the dashboard and fill in the details for your new task.
Can I access my notes from any device? ▶
Yes, AcademicFlow is web-based, so you can access your notes from any device with an internet connection.
How do I mark a task as completed? ▶
Go to your task list and click the checkbox next to the task you want to mark as completed.
Is my data secure? ▶
We use industry-standard security measures to keep your data safe and private.
How do I reset my password? ▶
On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions to reset your password.
